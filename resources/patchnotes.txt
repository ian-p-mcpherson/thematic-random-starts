Patchnotes 2.1

Compatability Changes
- Advanced options have been streamlined, now individual parts of the mod can be disabled:
  - Enable Thematic Robes : Toggle appearance changes
  - Enable Thematic Starts : Toggle loadout changes
  - Clear Inventory : Toggle clearing any starting items
  NOTE: The deprecated Passive mode is identical to disabling Robes and Loadouts

Balance Changes:
- More TNT for Construction Conjurer
- More rocks on Telekinetic
- Slightly slower chainsaw for Blood Cultist
- +10 HP for Vampire
- Faster shotgun for Berserker
- No bottle for Tidal Sorcerer
- +50 gold for Thrifty Shopper
- -10 HP for Tidal Sorcerer
